Our Story

GradGurus was founded in Edmonton, Alberta by two local young immigrants who believed students deserve clearer roadmaps, stronger mentorship, and faster paths to meaningful work.

We started by helping classmates navigate credit transfers, research milestones, and career prep. Today, we partner with students across programs to optimize degrees, publish with confidence, and connect with opportunities.
